About Us

Established in 1998, Cactus has grown from a beloved local secret into an iconic landmark on the Victoria Island waterfront. We take pride in offering a diverse, world-class menu that blends international flavors with the vibrant spirit of Lagos. Whether you're enjoying a sunset dinner on our lagoon-view terrace or a family brunch in our cozy dining room, we provide an atmosphere for every occasion. Our legendary in-house bakery continues to set the standard for artisanal treats, featuring our famous cakes and freshly baked breads.

At Cactus, we are dedicated to creating more than just meals—we create lasting memories through exceptional food and warm hospitality.
